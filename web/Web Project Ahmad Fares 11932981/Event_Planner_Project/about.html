Events by S. drawing smiles on children's faces, because memories are a lifetime.

Events by S.

Events by S. company takes the initiative of bringing back those joyous moments that grow distant by the day. In our modern rush of life and the age of internet more parents are having to send their kids to kinderGardens and after schools, while they're busy working full time to provide for their family. This makes the traditional celebrations and gathering all the more important for keeping and building a healthy family bond. The Challenge however is creating the space for making these much needed memories, and this is where we come in. Whether formal or informal and whatever the occasion, Events by S. has you covered. From Professional sets to little princess themes, we provide it all. With our own Professional team of sound designers and photographers, as well as a high end kitchen team for catering, we will turn any event into memorable smiles.
